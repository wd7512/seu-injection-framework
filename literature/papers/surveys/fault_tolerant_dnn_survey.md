# Survey on Fault-Tolerant Methodologies for Deep Neural Networks

**Polish Academy of Sciences**

## Summary

Extensive survey covering fault tolerance methodologies for DNNs in safety-critical applications.

## Categories of Fault Tolerance

### Hardware-Level
- Radiation-hardened components
- Error-correcting codes (ECC)
- Triple Modular Redundancy (TMR)

### Algorithm-Level
- Fault-aware training
- Robust architecture design
- Redundant computations

### Software-Level
- Runtime error detection
- Recovery mechanisms
- Checkpointing strategies

## Key Insights

- DNNs inherently have fault tolerance due to weight redundancy
- Critical vs. non-critical parameters can be identified
- Layer-wise vulnerability varies significantly
